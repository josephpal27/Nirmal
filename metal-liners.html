Metal Liners

Metal liners, crucial steel components in railway tracks, enhance structural integrity by distributing train weight evenly, maintaining track gauge, and reducing stress and deformation. This improves track stability, durability, and overall railway performance, minimizing the risk of derailments and ensuring smooth, efficient train operations.

FEATURES

Material

Metal liners are made using MS Billets.

Size and types

Metal liners are available in various configurations and drawing numbers such as T 3738, T 3740, T 3741, T 3742, T 8254, T 8255, T 8616, T 8617, T 8748, T 8749, T 8750.

Standards

Metal liners conform to RDSO Standards and are also available in customer specifications

APPLICATIONS

Track Renewal and Maintenance

Metal liners play a crucial role in the renewal and maintenance of railway tracks. When replacing worn-out or damaged rails, these liners ensure a smooth transition between the old and new sections, preventing stress concentrations and potential failures. During track maintenance, liners can be used to temporarily support the track structure, minimising disruptions to train services.

Curve Geometry

Metal liners optimise track geometry in curved sections. Precise rail alignment with the curve reduces wheel wear, improves ride quality, and increases train speeds. Additionally, they mitigate centrifugal forces acting on trains, enhancing safety and reducing derailment risks.

Vibration Damping

Metal liners have damping properties that reduce vibrations transmitted through the track structure. This improves passenger comfort and reduces noise pollution. They absorb shock and vibration, these liners extend the lifespan of the track infrastructure.

Thermal Expansion and Contraction Management

Metal liners accommodate rail expansion and contraction due to temperature fluctuations. This prevents buckling and other related issues, especially in regions with significant temperature variations. The flexible interface of these liners absorbs stresses caused by thermal expansion and contraction.

Enhanced Track Resilience

Metal liners enhance track resilience in areas prone to extreme weather conditions. As a protective barrier, these liners prevent water ingress and frost damage, ensuring long-term track integrity.
