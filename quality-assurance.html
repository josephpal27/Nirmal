QUALITY POLICY

We, at Nirmal Wires within the scope and boundary defined in the Quality Management System are committed to produce Quality Products to satisfy the needs and exceed and expectations of the customers, stakeholders and other interested parties and applicable requirements of the Management System by “Continual Improvement” in system and procedures.

At every stage of production, we prioritize quality to deliver wires that meet or exceed industry standards. Our commitment to quality assurance includes-:

Raw Material Inspection

Thorough inspection of the raw materials to ensure they meet our stringent quality requirements.

In-Process Testing

Regular testing during the manufacturing process to verify adherence to specifications.

Final Inspection

Comprehensive quality checks on finished wires, including dimensions, surface finish, and mechanical properties.

Certifications

Our products come with quality certifications to provide confidence in their performance.

To accomplish this we will –

  • Supply quality products and services for customers’ delight.
  • Document, implement, maintain and periodically review the effectiveness and performance of Quality Management System.
  • Comply with all relevant obligations applicable to products, activities, services and processes in respect of Quality.
  • Encourage employee involvement and participation in developmental activities.
  • Imparting education, training, awareness among the employees and contract workers for improving the level of consciousness related to Quality.
  • Ensure to make the policy available to all concerned for making them aware of their individual obligation towards “Quality Management System”.
