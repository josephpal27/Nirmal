LT AB Cables

LT Aerial Bunched (AB) Cables are versatile overhead power lines, ideal for various terrains and theft-prone areas. They offer lower installation costs than underground cables and are less susceptible to environmental factors. With customizable configurations for 1 and 3 phase systems and optional lighting conductors, they ensure efficient and reliable electricity distribution. The cables feature stranded or compacted aluminium conductors for phases and messenger wire, with PE or XLPE insulation. A separate neutral conductor can be added, and the messenger wire can double as earth and neutral.

FEATURES

Material

LT AB Cables are made using premium quality aluminium. Flexibility- Class 2 (as per IS 8130:1984)

Configuration

LT AB Cables are available in multiple core concentrically stranded configurations of 7 and 19 wires.

Standards

LT AB Cables conform to IS:14255-1995 up to the latest amendment.

Sizes and Insulation

LT AB Cables are available in various ranges of sizes. The insulation materials used include PE or XLPE (93% silane grafted polyethylene and 7% catalyst)

Voltage Rating

LT AB Cables are rated up to and including 1.1KV (1100 volts). Suitable for use on AC Single phase or 3 phase (earthed or unearthed) systems for rated voltages up to and including 1100 volts.

Packaging

LT AB Cables are packaged in drums according to IS 10418:1972. The end of the cable is sealed with non hygroscopic sealing material.

APPLICATIONS

Rural Electrification

LT AB Cables are particularly well-suited for rural electrification projects. Their compact design and ease of installation make them ideal for areas with dispersed populations and challenging terrains. They can be easily strung on existing poles or new structures, reducing the overall cost of electrification. Additionally, their superior performance in adverse weather conditions ensures reliable power supply to rural communities.

Urban Distribution Networks

In urban areas, LT AB Cables offer several advantages over traditional overhead lines. They have a lower visual impact and can be installed in congested areas with limited space. Their higher current-carrying capacity allows for efficient power distribution, reducing the need for additional infrastructure. Moreover, their reduced electromagnetic interference makes them suitable for areas with sensitive electronic equipment.

Industrial Applications

LT AB Cables are widely used in industrial settings to provide power to various machinery, equipment, and lighting systems. Their robust construction and high insulation levels make them resistant to mechanical stress, chemical exposure, and thermal shock. These cables can be installed in both indoor and outdoor environments, offering flexibility and reliability in industrial applications.

Transmission and Distribution Networks

LT AB Cables are employed in both transmission and distribution networks, depending on the voltage level and power requirements. They can be used for primary distribution, secondary distribution, and even tertiary distribution, providing a versatile solution for power delivery. Their ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions, such as high temperatures, humidity, and wind loads, ensures long-term performance.

Temporary Power Supply

LT AB Cables are often used for temporary power supply during construction, events, and emergency situations. Their quick and easy installation, along with their ability to handle high current loads, make them ideal for temporary power solutions. They can be easily dismantled and reused, providing a cost-effective and efficient power supply option.
