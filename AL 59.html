AL 59 Aluminium Conductor

AL 59 conductors are a type of high-conductivity, lightweight alloy conductor made from aluminium-magnesium-silicon (Al-Mg-Si) alloy. They offer superior electrical conductivity, high tensile strength, fatigue and corrosion resistance, making them a durable and efficient choice for power transmission and distribution.

FEATURES

Material

AL59 Conductors are made using AL 59 Alloy.

Standards

AL59 Conductors conform to IS 398(VI)

Strand Configuration

AL59 Conductors are available in various strand configurations which include 7,19,37,61

Packaging

AL 59 Cables are packaged in drums.

APPLICATIONS

Cable and Conductor Industry

AL 59 is used in manufacturing of AAAC and AACSR conductors. The alloy has inherent properties like increased strength, improved conductivity and superior corrosion resistance which contribute to the overall durability and performance of these conductors.
