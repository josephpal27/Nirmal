Aluminium Ingots

Aluminium ingots are the raw form of Aluminium that is cast into standard shapes for further processing. They are widely used in various industries due to their versatility, lightweight nature, and corrosion resistance. These ingots serve as the foundation for manufacturing Aluminium products like Ingots, T-Ingots, Sows Ingots,

Features

Lightweight

Aluminium is very light weight which makes it ideal for applications where weight is a crucial factor.

Corrosion Resistance

Aluminium exhibits excellent corrosion resistance due to a protective oxide layer which forms on its surface when corrosion starts, shielding it from further damage.

High Thermal and Electrical Conductivity

Aluminium displays high thermal and electrical conductivity. This makes it suitable for various electrical applications.
