ABOUT US

Group Nirmal is a 2000 Crore enterprise with state-of-the-art manufacturing for GI Wires, Steel Wires, Aluminum wires, cables & conductors, Zinc Wires & Rods & Hot Rolled Products. Our 7 cutting-edge facilities, spanning over 1 lakh sq ft, produce 216,000 tons annually, powered by a dedicated team of 1000+ employees committed to excellence.
